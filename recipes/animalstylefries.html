In-n-Out's Animal Style Fries Recipe

Description

everyone knows about innout's not-so-secret menu. and everyone knows about the one and only, spectacular, delicious, delectable, animal style fries. however, we also all know that innout can be stingy as to where they're located, so if you live in an area without an innout, check out this recipe to learn about how you can try these life-changing fries for yourself. it's easier than you think!

Ingredients

Spread Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle radish
  • 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar

Grilled Onions

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 4 1/2 cups chopped yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons water, divided

Fries

  • 1 (28 ounce) package frozen shoestring potato fries
  • 4 slices American Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Spread: Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, relish, sugar, and vinegar in a small bowl until well combined. Cover and set aside until serving.
  2. Prepare the Grilled Onions: Heat butter and oil in a large skillet over medium. Add onions and salt; cook, stirring occasionally, until softened and golden brown, about 20 minutes. When skillet starts to become dry, add 1 tablespoon of water. Continue cooking, stirring often, adding more water, 1 tablespoon at a time, as needed, until onions are very soft, deeply browned, and jammy, 10 to 15 more minutes (you may not need to use all the water). Cover and set aside to keep warm.
  3. Prepare fries according to package instructions.
  4. Divide fries evenly among 4 plates; immediately top with 1 slice cheese, 3 tablespoons grilled onions, and 3 tablespoons spread.
